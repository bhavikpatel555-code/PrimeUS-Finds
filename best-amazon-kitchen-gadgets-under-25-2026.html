2. Digital Kitchen Scale

A digital kitchen scale helps you measure ingredients accurately, making it easier to follow recipes and control portion sizes. Whether you're baking cakes, preparing healthy meals, or tracking calories, a reliable kitchen scale improves consistency and reduces food waste.

Most affordable Amazon models feature a clear LCD display, multiple measurement units, and a tare function that lets you measure ingredients inside bowls or containers. It's a simple gadget that quickly becomes an everyday kitchen essential.
