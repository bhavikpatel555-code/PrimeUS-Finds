Frequently Asked Questions

Which highlighter is best for students?

Sharpie Clear View Highlighters and Zebra Mildliner Highlighters are among the most popular choices because of their smooth ink flow, bright colors, and excellent writing quality.

Do highlighters bleed through notebook paper?

Most quality highlighters are designed to minimize bleed-through, but thicker or lower-quality paper may still show some ink on the opposite side.

Are pastel highlighters better for studying?

Many students prefer pastel highlighters because they are easier on the eyes during long study sessions while still making important notes stand out.
